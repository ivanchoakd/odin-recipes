Ceviche

Ingredients

  • Ocean fish
  • Limes
  • Red onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeno peppers
  • Avocado

How to make ceviche (step by step)

  1. Slice the onions and place in a bowl. Add the salt and lime juice and let these marinate while you continue
  2. Cut the fish into 1/2-inch to 3/4-inch cubes and toss them with the marinating onions.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients- cucumber, tomatoes and cilantro and give a good toss. If adding avocado, wait until serving to add it.
  4. Refrigerate for 45-60 minutes. The longer you let the fish cure in the marinade, the more “cooked” through the ceviche will become. 45 minutes is perfect.
  5. Serve and enjoy
