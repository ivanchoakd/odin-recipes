Cesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Shreded cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Cesars Dressing
  • croutons

How to make a cesar salad (step by step)

  1. In a large bowl add the chopped lettuce with enough dressing to lightly coat the leaves
  2. Add half of the croutons and Parmesan cheese and toss again.
  3. Top with remaining croutons and cheese and serve immediately.
