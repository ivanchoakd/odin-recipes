Matambre a la pizza

Ingredients

  • matambre
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • milk
  • onion
  • ham
  • olives
  • bell pepper
  • garlic

How to make matambre a la pizza (step by step)

  1. Boil the matambre in milk for 2 hours on low heat
  2. Slice the onion, bell pepper and the garlic then cook them with a little olive oil,then add the tomato sauce, salt and pepper to taste
  3. Once the matambre is ready, put it on a oven plate with the tomato sauce and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add the ham and cheese and let it grate for 5 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
